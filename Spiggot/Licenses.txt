========================================
libgphoto2 / libgphoto2_port and friends
========================================
This app bundles dynamically-linked libraries and camera driver plugins from the
libgphoto2 ecosystem (including camera “camlibs”). These components are
licensed under the GNU Lesser General Public License, version 2.1 (LGPL-2.1).

Common bundled components may include (depending on platform/build):
- libgphoto2
- libgphoto2_port
- libusb
- libexif
- libintl (gettext)
- libltdl (GNU libtool)

Where required by the LGPL, you are permitted to replace these shared libraries
with your own modified versions (so long as they remain ABI-compatible), and to
reverse engineer for the purpose of debugging modifications.

Upstream sources:
- libgphoto2: https://github.com/gphoto/libgphoto2
- libusb: https://github.com/libusb/libusb
- libexif: https://github.com/libexif/libexif
- gettext (libintl): https://www.gnu.org/software/gettext/
- GNU libtool (libltdl): https://www.gnu.org/software/libtool/
